how to Encrypt using the Caesar Cipher:

1. Enter in the amount you wish to shift the alphabet by. For Exemple: A with shift 3 is D

2. Enter in the word you wish to encrypt and then click encrypt. The encrypted message will be displayed below

how to Decrypt using the Caesar Cipher:

1. Enter in the shift that was used to encrypt the message

2. Enter in the encypted message you wish to decrypt and click decrypt. The decrypted message will be displayed below
